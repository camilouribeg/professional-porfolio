01 — The Starting Point

One flat table.
No analytics layer.

Pentana ERA Power is a dealership management system used by approximately 80% of Australian new-car franchise dealerships. The raw data export was a single flat table — 8.7M rows, 143 columns, no structure optimised for analytics. Of those rows, 4.9M represent unique new vehicle VINs, which is the working dataset for this project.

A staged pipeline built in Snowflake SQL and Python. The key design decision was splitting the Silver layer into five focused analytical tables rather than cleaning one wide flat table — each purpose-built for a downstream use case.

The full Pentana ERA Power export preserved as-is. Source of truth — no transformations applied. The complete history of ~8.7M rows across all vehicle types is retained here.

03 — AI Enrichment Layer

Filling the gaps the DMS can't answer.

With the analytics foundation in place, the project moved into an AI-assisted enrichment layer — combining web scraping, API decoding, and external datasets to unlock dealer intelligence that isn't captured in the raw sales data.

VIN Decoding & Vehicle Enrichment
The source data has inconsistent vehicle descriptors — make, body style, fuel type, and seats are partially missing or incorrectly labelled. A two-phase VIN decoding pipeline (WMI-based + WMI+VDS) enriched 4.9M VINs using the NHTSA vPIC API and a curated internal WMI dictionary.

VIN Enrichment Report ↗
Dealer Name & Identity
The raw data identifies dealers only by postcode + brand — no names or addresses. A web research pipeline using Tavily API and Google Custom Search resolved verified trading names, street addresses, and match confidence for 2,034 dealer proxy keys (63.4% verified).
Complete
Dealer Reference Report ↗
Dealer Group Taxonomy
Automotive dealers operate within ownership groups (e.g. Eagers Automotive, Servco, Peter Warren). A multi-tier resolution pipeline — web-verified lookups, name-prefix clustering, algorithmic multi-site grouping — mapped 329 named groups across 2,034 dealers, covering 74.1% of 4.9M VINs.
Complete
Dealer Group Report ↗
Dealer Land Size
Physical lot size (sqm) is a proxy for a dealer's stock capacity and operational scale. Cadastral parcel data was queried for each dealer address via land registry lookups. East-coast pilot covers NSW and Queensland.
In progress — NSW & QLD pilot complete
Land Size Report ↗
RRP Reference Data
Recommended retail prices (RRP) per make, model, year, and variant enable revenue potential estimates, discount rate benchmarking, and premium variant mix analysis — covering 100% of vehicles without relying on the partially-populated sale price field (~33% coverage). Being built by the wider team.
Team workstream In progress
VFACTS Market Data
VFACTS is the FCAI's monthly new vehicle registration dataset — the industry standard for market share in Australia. The first step is column standardisation — aligning VFACTS make/model naming to Pentana's conventions. Once mapped, it unlocks true market share calculations, white space analysis, and growth-vs-market comparisons by brand and geography.

04 — Benchmarking Framework

Defining the dealer
benchmarking framework.

With the data platform established, the next stage is defining a comprehensive benchmarking framework — analysing which dealer performance variables are computable from the current pipeline, which require external enrichment, and which need new data sources entirely.

Volume & Growth
Year-on-year growth, rolling 12M units, peak-month concentration (end-of-quarter sales culture signal), volume rank within brand.
Operational Speed
Order-to-delivery median & P75, OTD consistency score (stddev), on-time rate, estimated delivery accuracy, yard time velocity.
Portfolio & Electrification
SUV/ute/passenger share, aged stock rate, EV & hybrid share, premium variant mix, segment diversity index.
Pricing & Revenue
Gross margin, discount rate, price realisation rate vs RRP, RRP revenue potential (100% coverage), discount vs brand average.
Geographic & Competitive
Competitive density (same-brand dealers within 50km), nearest competitor distance, catchment SEIFA decile, metro/regional flag.
Market Share (VFACTS)
Market share in state, national brand share, growth vs total market, brand segment mix vs national average. Unlocked once VFACTS is available.

08 — The Goal

Turning raw DMS data into
dealer intelligence.

The platform is a means to an end. The objective is to give Synogize — and ultimately the dealerships — a clear, data-driven picture of how each dealer is performing, why, and what to do about it.

📍
Know where you stand
Every dealer gets a benchmarked view of their own performance — order-to-delivery speed, EV mix, discount rate, volume growth — ranked within their brand and region, not in isolation.
🔍
Spot the white space
Combining Pentana sales with VFACTS registration data reveals where a brand is under-represented relative to the market — surfacing geographic opportunities that aren't visible from internal data alone.
📈
Drive better decisions
From OEM strategy to dealer operations — the benchmarking framework is designed to answer the questions that matter: who is outperforming, what's driving it, and which levers can be pulled to close the gap.

The foundation is now in place — a clean, enriched, production-grade dataset covering an estimated 80% of the Australian new-car market. The next step is translating it into dashboards, reports, and alerts that a dealer principal can act on Monday morning.
